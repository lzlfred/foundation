Our Mission

Connecting young people, oceans, and inner well-being

Liu Horizon Foundation intends to protect and restore the ocean's natural ecosystems while nurturing human inner peace and wellness and educating the younger generation.

We focus on teaching young people about the climate and empowering them to maintain healthy ecosystems. All programs connect participants with nature while fostering self-awareness, compassion, and environmental responsibility.

Programs

How we carry out our mission

Ocean-Themed Mindfulness Retreats

We host ocean-themed mindfulness retreats that combine time in coastal environments with guided practices led by qualified yoga, meditation, and wellness instructors. Participants explore how inner calm and compassion support long-term care for the planet.

Coastal Cleanups & Wellness Events

We organize local coastal cleanup events, often paired with community wellness activities and educational sessions on ocean health, climate impacts, and individual actions that make a difference.

Qingdao international students seminar and education

Collaborate with Qingdao voluntery study-aboard organzation to host seminar and education programs for high school students to learn information, study aboard (eg, the U.S) and share career opportunities for college and graduate school students.

Partnerships with Ocean-Focused NGOs & Institutions

We may partner with ocean-focused NGOs or marine science institutions to co-host awareness events or fund educational materials that advance shared goals around ocean conservation and youth education.

Where We Work

Coastal regions vulnerable to ocean & climate challenges

Liu Horizon Foundation focuses on coastal areas that are vulnerable to climate or ocean-related challenges, and communities affected by environmental degradation.

  • Coastal regions in California
  • Selected coastal areas in China
  • Selected coastal areas in Southeast Asia

Specific locations and partnerships are determined based on community need, environmental impact, and program feasibility.

Our Approach to International Work

When working internationally, the Foundation follows U.S. legal requirements and best practices to ensure that funds and activities are used solely for charitable purposes. We perform careful due diligence and monitoring of foreign partners and projects.

Grants & Partnerships

Supporting aligned organizations

From time to time, Liu Horizon Foundation may make grants to ocean-focused NGOs or marine science institutions to co-host awareness events or fund educational materials that align with our mission.

Eligibility & Alignment

Potential partner organizations must demonstrate:

  • A mission consistent with ocean protection and youth education
  • Capacity to carry out the proposed program or project
  • Transparent financial practices and reporting

How We Steward Funds

Before funding, we request proposals describing mission alignment, project plans, budgets, and expected outcomes. After funds are distributed, partners are asked to provide detailed reports and documentation of how funds were used.

The Board of Directors maintains records of all distributions and may request additional information or site visits. If funds are misused or appear to be misused, partners are expected to return any misapplied amounts.

At this time, primary funding is provided by the Foundation’s directors.
